Not registered yet? Get immediate access without a security code

You can get limited service immediately, similar to a Guest account by beginning the registration process and proving your identity. You do not need to finish the full registration process (mail or call for a security code, set up security questions etc.) to access the following:

  • View status of your tax return
  • Notification preferences
  • Request a remittance voucher
  • View notice of assessment or reassessment (simple view, lines X, Y and Z)
  • View RRSP deduction limit and TFSA contribution room
  • Pay by pre-authorized debit (create new agreement)

Try My Account as a guest

Option 2 – Using a CRA user ID and password

Log in with your CRA user ID and password, or register.

CRA login   CRA register

My Account is a secure portal that lets you set up your profile, view your personal income tax and benefit status, and manage your tax affairs online. This service is available 21 hours a day.

You can set up a payment plan through My Account. To learn about payments, go to Payments to the Canada Revenue Agency.
