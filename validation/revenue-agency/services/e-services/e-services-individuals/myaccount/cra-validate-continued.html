If you live in Canada or the United States

Postal code or ZIP code

This process may include the mailing of a CRA security code to your address currently on file with the CRA.

If you are blind or partially sighted, you can ask to receive your personal correspondence from the CRA in an alternate format.

If your mailing address changed, it is important that we have your new mailing address on file. You must contact us to give us your new address before you continue with this process.

The postal code or ZIP code field is not case sensitive and may or may not include spaces or dashes.

Tax information

You must use your copy of the return for the tax year specified.

Enter the dollar amount of the line specified. This must be the amount you calculated and entered on your return.

If the amount that you calculated is negative, enter the value with a minus sign (for example, enter negative 200 as "-200"). If the amount is zero, enter it as "0". When you enter the amount, do not use commas, spaces, periods, or cents.
