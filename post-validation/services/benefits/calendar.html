Benefits payment dates

Dates for recurring Government of Canada benefit payments, including some provincial and territorial benefit programs administered by the Canada Revenue Agency. If you have signed up for direct deposit, payments will be automatically deposited in your bank account on these dates.

  • Veterans disability pension
  • Canada child benefit
  • Working income tax benefit
  • Goods and services tax / harmonized sales tax credit (GST/HST credit)
  • Ontario trillium benefit (OTB)
